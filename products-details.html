      PRODUCT BRIEF INTRODUCTION

      Refrigerant Gas R404A

      R404A is a refrigerant widely used in automotive air conditioning, refrigerators, central air conditioning, industrial and commercial refrigerators, and in medicine, pesticides, cosmetics and detergents as catalysts, flame retardants and foaming agents.

      Refrigerant gas R404a

      It's a colourless, non-flammable gas at atmospheric pressure with a slight ethereal odour. Commerial and medium temperature refrigeration applications, It is widely used in cold storage, food refrigeration equipment, marine refrigeration equipment, industrial refrigeration, commercial refrigeration, transportation refrigerating equipment (refrigerated vehicles), supermarket refrigeration condensing unit, display cabinets and other refrigeration equipment

      Product Description

      The product is used as refrigerant in household, industrial and commercial air-conditioning system. It can also be used as aerosol propellant for pesticide and paint, or fire extinguishing agent. It is the primary feedstock for Fluoropolymers.

      Physical & chemical properties:

      Under normal temperature and pressure, the product is a colorless, odorless and nontoxic gas. its boiling point is-51.53°C . Its density is 1.213g/cm3 at 20°C. Its mixture with air is nonflammable and free of explosion risk. It shows good thermal and chemical stability under normal conditions.

      Storage & transportation:

      The product is packed in appropriate cylinders of tanks (or tank cars). It is to keep in cool and dry place, without being exposed to heat source, sunlight and raining. It is to comply with the regulations of railway and road transportation of China concerning hazardous cargo.
